Lounge Bar

Whether it's Aperitif, apres ski, nightcap or a cozy get together - enjoy our creative cocktails, high quality wines availabe by the glass, refreshing beer and carefully selected spirits in an exceptional atmosphere. Our kitchen team from the fine dining restaurant After Seven by Ivo Adam, will satisfy your light hunger with delicious and always freshly prepared snacks until 11:00PM

Valet Parking

Hillside Hotel offers a professional valet company to relieve you of the hassle of parking your vehicle on arrival, with service from 7:00 am until 10.00 pm. Your vehicle will be transferred to safe and secured underground parking five minutes’ walk from the hotel. Should you need your car during your stay, an extra €10 will be applied for the return service. Please inform the reception desk at least two hours ahead of time when you want your car brought to you. If you need your car before or after available hours, please contact our reception desk.

Fitness Center

Maintain your fitness routine with our state-of-the-art fitness center equipped with Precor and True Cardio machines complete with integrated LCD panels, Paramount strength training machines and free weights. Our Peloton bikes have built-in virtual reality routes to enhance and motivate your workout experience. Fitness center amenities include cooling towels, bottled water and complimentary headphones.

Swimming Pool

Hillside Hotel’s swimming pool is set with a perfect venting system, strict water quality control, and a luxury pool surrounded with natural light ensuring a relaxing atmosphere. Enjoy your swim in Hillside Hotel’s indoor swimming pool, regardless of weather or external conditions.

Single Room

Are you looking for a comfortable stay in San Francisco? The Standard Single Room has the perfect facilities to give you a pleasant night after spending a long day in the city. The special designed Corendon Single bed, dressed in pure white cotton, topped with soft pillows, a lightweight duvet and the blackout curtains ensure you a perfect night’s sleep.

Queen Room

Our luxurious Queen rooms offer an affordable, comfortable and convenient place to unwind after an adventurous day and exciting night in San Francisco. These bright, plush rooms include contemporary furnishings, luxurious linens, fully-equipped bathrooms, and HD televisions.

Double Queen Room

Double Room is designed not only for single travelers and couples, but also for families with children. Rooms have a Queen Size bed or Twins that may be joined together. In addition, it is possible to accommodate an extra bed for a child or an adult. The room is decorated with style and attention to every detail.

King Room

For a luxury hotel room that offers comfort and convenience with an extra touch of class, our King rooms provide the perfect getaway. These beautifully appointed rooms come with luxurious furnishings, bright, modern décor and everyday conveniences like a flat-screen TV and WiFi access.

Deluxe King Room

The Deluxe King room is your place to unwind and refresh in grand style. In addition to a decadently comfortable bed with luxurious fabrics, flat screen TV entertainment, and spa-inspired bathroom retreat - these deluxe rooms also offer an additional lounge area for you to spread out and relax while enjoying your stay at Hillside Hotel.
